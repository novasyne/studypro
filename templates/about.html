About Novasyne

Pioneering the Future of Healthcare Technology

Novasyne is a research-driven technology company based in Brisbane, Australia, pioneering breakthroughs at the intersection of Neuroscience, Mental Health and Artificial Intelligence.

Founded on the belief that cutting-edge research should translate into real-world solutions, we bridge the gap between academic discovery and practical application in healthcare and neuroscience.

We envision a future where advanced AI seamlessly integrates into healthcare, providing clinicians and researchers with powerful tools to understand, predict, and treat neurological and mental health conditions. Our research-first approach ensures that every solution we develop is grounded in rigorous scientific methodology and validated through peer-reviewed publications.

We believe that innovation should be inclusive. We are dedicated to making our tools and services accessible to disadvantaged communities and developing nations, offering them at low or no cost whenever possible. Our goal is to ensure that the benefits of neuroscience and AI innovation extend beyond borders and economic barriers.
